| 6 | `Deployer already has ROLE_COMMUNITY` | 幂等 | 设计正确 |
| 7 | `Deployer operator already configured` | 幂等 | 设计正确 |
| 8 | `getAgentSponsorshipRate not in 5.3.1` | **V5.4+ 待落地** | V5.4 unskip |
| 9 | `OPERATOR_ADDRESS not set` | 环境变量未设 | 文档化或脚本默认值 |
| 10 | `setAgentPolicies not in 5.3.1` | **V5.4+ 待落地** | V5.4 unskip |
