## What SuperPaymaster is (and what it isn't)

SuperPaymaster is **not a wallet**. It's the layer that decides **who pays for gas and how a
payment settles** — on top of any ERC-4337 v0.7 account. In the AAStar stack the labor splits
cleanly:

- **AirAccount (account layer)** — WHO can sign and WITH what limits: WebAuthn/passkey, Session
  Keys (target / selector / velocity / quota), tiered multisig, social recovery, agent delegation.
- **SuperPaymaster (settlement layer)** — WHO pays gas and HOW it settles: gasless sponsorship,
  xPNTs credit/debt, reputation pricing, x402 + micropayment-channel settlement.

This is why SuperPaymaster does **not** re-implement spending-limits or session logic — AirAccount
already enforces those at the account. SuperPaymaster sponsors and settles. Together they form a
full community payment stack; neither duplicates the other.

|---|------------|-------------------------|
| 1 | **Gasless gas sponsorship** | Users hold zero ETH; gas is paid in a community token (xPNTs). The community (operator) pre-funds aPNTs. |

## 🔒 Security-hardened: 6 audit fixes

This beta exists to close a security audit. Each finding was implemented → adversarially
reviewed by Codex → fixed → re-reviewed until clean → verified against the live contracts:

| ID | Severity | Fix |
|----|----------|-----|
| C-01 | Critical | Balance-aware credit ceiling — over-limit users without xPNTs balance are rejected |
| C-02 | Critical | x402 direct settle now requires a signed EIP-712 `X402PaymentAuthorization` (no unsigned drain of any holder's xPNTs) |
| C-03 | Critical | EIP-3009 nonce binds the recipient (`keccak256(payee, salt)`) — a facilitator can't redirect funds |
| C-04 | Critical | postOp out-of-gas floor (`MIN_POST_OP_GAS` + validation buffer) |
| H-01 | High | Chunked `retryPendingDebt` — bounded debt settlement |
| H-02 | High | PoP-gated permissionless BLS registration behind a switch (default OFF) |

**Verification:** 979/0 forge unit tests · 35/35 E2E green against the live Sepolia contracts
(100% ABI coverage) · UUPS-upgradeable with preserved storage. Full report:
[`docs/coverage-report-2026-06-02.md`](https://github.com/AAStarCommunity/SuperPaymaster/blob/main/docs/coverage-report-2026-06-02.md).

## 🚀 Getting started (integrators)

**Step 1 — meet the two prerequisites.** Most first failures are an opaque `AA34`. They come
from exactly two things, both avoidable:
- the account must be **eligible** (SBT holder or registered agent), and
- the **price cache must be fresh**, the operator funded, and the user's credit not exhausted.

**Step 2 — pre-flight with `dryRunValidation`.** It returns the exact reason code on-chain
(`STALE_PRICE` / `INSUFFICIENT_BALANCE` / `CREDIT_EXCEEDED` / `USER_NOT_ELIGIBLE`), so you never
burn a UserOp on a predictable revert.

**Step 3 — sponsor.** Build the v0.7 `PackedUserOperation`, set `paymasterAndData`, give postOp a
real gas budget (the C-04 fix enforces a 200k floor), and submit.

Full walkthrough with viem snippets: the
[Beta Integration Guide](https://github.com/AAStarCommunity/SuperPaymaster/blob/main/docs/integration/v5.3.3-beta.2-integration-guide.md).

> x402: the full HTTP-402 consumer flow needs the SDK signing update (#39). Server-to-server
> self-settle works today via the SDK; raw EIP-712 reference implementations are in
> `script/gasless-tests/test-x402-*.js`.
