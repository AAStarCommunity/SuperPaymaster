## What is ERC-8004?

ERC-8004 (also known as **EIP-8004**) is an Ethereum Improvement Proposal officially named **"Trustless Agents"**.

Its core goal is to provide **Autonomous AI Agents** with a unified standard for discovery, identity verification, and interaction in decentralized environments, establishing a trust layer without pre-existing trust.

### Core Mechanism: Three On-chain Registries

ERC-8004 introduces three lightweight on-chain registries that work together to form the foundation of a trustless agent economy:

1. **Identity Registry**
   - **Purpose:** Provides each AI agent with a **portable, censorship-resistant unique identifier**
   - **Technical Foundation:** Built on **ERC-721** (NFT standard). Each agent's identity is an ownable, transferable NFT, immediately compatible with existing NFT wallets and marketplaces
   - **Contents:** Stores agent's `AgentID` (ERC-721 token), `Token URI` pointing to agent metadata, and communication endpoints

2. **Reputation Registry**
   - **Purpose:** Provides agents with a **public, transparent feedback mechanism**
   - **Contents:** Allows clients to publish feedback after task completion, including score (0-100), optional tags, and links to off-chain detailed feedback data with hash verification
   - **Goal:** Enables other agents or users to decide whether to trust and select an agent based on public reputation data

3. **Validation Registry**
   - **Purpose:** Provides a **formalized third-party verification mechanism** for high-risk scenarios
   - **Mechanism:** Standard interface for **requesting and recording independent validator verification of agent work results**
   - **Goal:** Ensures payments or other high-value operations execute only after work is verified through crypto-economic security models or cryptographic validation (e.g., Restaking integration)

### Why It Matters

- **Enables Open AI Agent Economy:** Allows AI agents from different organizations and ecosystems to securely discover, collaborate, and transact without pre-existing trust
- **Bridges Trust Gap:** Solves the lack of pre-existing trust between agents in decentralized networks through combination of on-chain identity, reputation, and validation
- **Web3 Integration:** Leverages ERC-721 and Ethereum's permissionless nature to deeply integrate AI agents into the current Web3 ecosystem

**In short, ERC-8004 aims to become the "ERC-20" for AI agents, laying the foundation for trust and interoperability in decentralized AI agent economies.**

## 什么是 ERC-8004？

EIP-8004（或称 **ERC-8004**）是一个以太坊改进提案，正式名称为 **"Trustless Agents"（无需信任的代理）** 标准。

它的核心目标是为**自治 AI 代理（Autonomous AI Agents）**提供一套在去中心化环境中进行发现、身份验证和交互的统一标准，从而在没有预先信任的情况下建立信任层。

### 核心机制：三个链上注册表

ERC-8004 引入了三个轻量级的链上注册表，它们协同工作，构成了无需信任的代理经济体的基础：

1. **身份注册表 (Identity Registry)**
   - **作用：** 为每个 AI 代理提供一个**可移植、抗审查的唯一标识符**
   - **技术基础：** 基于 **ERC-721**（NFT 标准）构建。这意味着每个代理的身份都是一个可拥有、可转移的 NFT，使其能够立即与现有的 NFT 钱包和市场兼容
   - **内容：** 存储代理的 `AgentID` (ERC-721 token)、指向代理元数据文件的 `Token URI`，以及通信端点

2. **声誉注册表 (Reputation Registry)**
   - **作用：** 为代理提供一个**公开、透明的反馈机制**
   - **内容：** 允许客户在代理完成任务后发布反馈信号，包括分数（0-100）、可选标签以及指向链下详细反馈数据的链接及其哈希值（以确保数据完整性）
   - **目的：** 使其他代理或用户可以根据公开的声誉数据来决定是否信任和选择该代理

3. **验证注册表 (Validation Registry)**
   - **作用：** 为高风险场景提供一个**形式化的第三方验证机制**
   - **机制：** 提供一个标准接口，用于**请求和记录独立验证者对代理工作结果的验证**
   - **目的：** 确保支付或其他高价值操作只有在工作被加密经济学安全模型或密码学验证成功后才能执行（例如，与 Restaking 服务集成）

### 为什么它很重要？

- **实现开放的 AI 代理经济：** 允许来自不同组织和生态系统的 AI 代理安全、无需信任地相互发现、协作和交易
- **弥合信任鸿沟：** 在去中心化网络中，通过链上身份、声誉和验证的组合，解决了代理之间缺乏预先信任的问题
- **与 Web3 基础设施集成：** 利用 ERC-721 和以太坊的无需许可特性，将 AI 代理深度整合到当前的 Web3 生态系统中

**简而言之，ERC-8004 旨在成为 AI 代理的"ERC-20"，为去中心化 AI 代理经济奠定信任和互操作性的基础。**
